How Does It work?

This calculator's purpose is to find how long it would take for someone to retire based on their salary, savings/expenses, and the usual 5% and 4% Withdrawal Rate.

First is to find out how much you would need to retire we can find this out by the 4% Rule, or multipling your expenses by 25. Then we can exponentially compound the savings by the rate of return until you reach that 4% rule. Then you're retired!
